Projects

  • Structure Learning for Cosmos Monitoring:

    Summer Internship, Applied Sciences Group,
    Microsoft Research India.

    In this project, we learnt the structure in time series data. We then use this structures to detect the service issues as quockly as possible and identify the variables relevant for investigating the issue. Time series data is counter data generated in Microsft's Cosmos Monitoring System. We fit a gaussian graphical model to the time series data. To detect the service issues we look for the structural changes in this graphical model. We also use low probability events in this models to detect the service issues. We use linear dependency nets with L1 penalty for determining the gaussian models.

  • Polyphonic Music Transcription:

    Course Project: Artificial Intelligence

    In polyphonic music many notes are played at once. In this project we learnt 88 binary classifiers which helps in transcribing notes of polyphonic music. Each classifier detects the presence of one note in the music at every time step. Unspervised feature learning using RNN-RBM (Recursive Neural Networks and Restricted Boltzmann Machine). SVM classifiers are build using one-vs-all classification. HMM smoothing was done to improve the results of SVM classification. Click here to look at the poster for more details.
